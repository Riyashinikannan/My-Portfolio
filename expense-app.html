Expense tracker app

Introduction

The Expense Tracker App is a mobile application designed to help users manage their finances effectively by tracking daily expenses and income. This app is particularly beneficial for college students and young professionals who wish to monitor and optimize their spending habits. The app aims to provide users with a simple and intuitive platform to record, categorize, and analyze their financial activities, fostering better financial management and savings. Empower users to manage finances effectively. Encourage saving habits through goal tracking.Provide a clear view of spending patterns to make informed financial decisions. The Expense Tracker App is a practical tool for promoting financial literacy and helping users achieve their financial goals effortlessly.

Project Features

  • Log daily expenses and categorize them into groups like food, shopping, and groceries
  • Set spending limits for categories to avoid overspending.
  • Visualize expenses, income, and savings through charts and graphs.
  • Plan and track progress toward specific financial goals.
  • View detailed financial summaries for specific periods.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend and Backend: Developed using Flutter, offering a cross-platform, user-friendly experience.
  • UI/UX Design: Created with Figma, ensuring intuitive navigation and appealing visuals.
  • Data Analysis Tools: Integrates Excel, SQL, and Tableau for advanced insights and visualization.
